06 — FAQ

Got
Questions

What happens if something breaks? +
All products include 30 days of free support after delivery. After that, annual support plans start at R5,000/year. Most issues are resolved within 24 hours via remote session. For critical failures, we aim for same-day response.
How long does a typical project take? +
Standard website packages: 5–21 days depending on complexity. Custom builds: 2–6 weeks. We give fixed timelines upfront — no surprises. If we say 5 days, we deliver in 5 days.
Do I need to train my staff to use this? +
No. The whole point is software that works without consultants or training. We deliver with a simple walkthrough. If a feature needs documentation, we write it. Your team uses it from day one.
Can you work with our existing systems? +
Yes. Most engineering firms already use AutoCAD, Civil 3D, Trello, WhatsApp, and municipal portals. We build bridges to these systems. If your data lives somewhere, we can connect to it.
How is pricing structured? +
Fixed price, agreed upfront before any work begins. No hourly billing, no scope creep, no surprises. You know exactly what you pay before we write a single line of code.
